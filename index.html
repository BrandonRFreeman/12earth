Our Philosophy

Earthbound materials, celestial results.

Founded in Seattle, 12earth champions artists who bend molten glass into transcendent forms. Each piece is meticulously sourced for museums, collectors, and design studios seeking luminous statements that feel both modern and timeless.

From intimate vessels to monumental light compositions, we collaborate hand-in-hand with clients to curate work that reflects a specific space, emotion, and story.
